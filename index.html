Lilac October is an indie-rock band formed in 2018. It is a two-member band, Kleopatra Koutsourou (vocalist) and Kostis Kyriakou (guitarist, bassist). The main theme is the contrast between summer and winter, the nostalgic vibes of early childhood and back-stabbing friendships. You can listen to our music in every music service (Spotify, iTunes etc.)
